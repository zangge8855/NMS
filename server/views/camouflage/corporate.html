在线检测单元Inline inspection cell

集成相机、光源、治具与边缘推理模块，在产线节拍内完成判定与结果输出。 Cameras, lighting, fixtures, and edge inference modules are combined to complete inspection within production takt time.

边缘分析工作站Edge analytics station

统一归档缺陷图像、批次参数与复核记录，支持快速回溯和工艺比对。 Defect imagery, batch parameters, and review records are archived together for fast trace-back and process comparison.

自动化协同接口Automation interface

联动 PLC、MES 与机械手，实现剔除、复判、节拍同步与异常留痕。 Links with PLC, MES, and robotics for reject handling, rechecks, takt synchronization, and exception logging.

现场评估Site assessment

围绕节拍、来料差异、光学条件和治具空间完成快速勘查与方案收敛。 Cycle time, incoming variation, optics, and fixture space are evaluated to converge on a workable deployment plan.

联调实施Commissioning

完成设备上架、工艺参数整定、通信联调和生产节拍验证。 Deployment includes installation, parameter tuning, communications integration, and production takt validation.

培训巡检Training and inspection

针对操作、复判和维护岗位提供培训，并按周期执行远程或现场巡检。 Operator, review, and maintenance teams receive training with scheduled remote or on-site inspection support.
